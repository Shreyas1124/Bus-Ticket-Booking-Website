About Us

AirBus-Ticket booking platform

AirBus, has strived to deliver easy booking solutions to its customers. Our continued efforts have resulted into AirBus becoming one of the leading ticketing platforms in India for various Bus services. We have a strong presence with a ticket inventory from over 50 bus partners and 1000 route options our app and website.Founded in the year 2022, AirBus is a pioneer in providing end-to-end software and other value-added solutions such as e-ticketing systems, fleet management solutions, vehicle tracking systems, passenger information systems, logistics management backed by a 24x7 customer support center. AirBus is an ISO 9001:2022 certified company.
